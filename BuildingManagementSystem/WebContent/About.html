ABOUT

Vistaas a premium apartments enclave located off Bannerghatta road is being developed by Sona Valliappa Group. The project offers an exemplary lifestyle with a privilege to live in harmonious surroundings with unmatched convenience. The aesthetically designed and smartly planned project offers a great living experience with state of the art club house which talks about lifestyle features and amenities. Elegantly crafted 1, 2, and 3 BHK homes offer a superior and efficient design that provides more living space keeping in mind the true parameters of a lifestyle that is large. Its a low rise project designed to reduce the concrete footprint thus offering a large open and green space making one feel closer to the nature.
